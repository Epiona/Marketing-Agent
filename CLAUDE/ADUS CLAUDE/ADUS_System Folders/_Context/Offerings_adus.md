**OVERVIEW**

ADUS Klinik is a privately operated specialty hospital in Dielsdorf,
Canton Zurich. The clinic offers both outpatient (ambulatory) and
inpatient (stationary) treatment across a focused range of medical
disciplines. The core offering is built around orthopaedics, sports
medicine, traumatology, and surgery --- always with the patient\'s
individual needs at the centre.

The clinic operates 3 operating theatres and 20 acute-care beds.
Consultations have been offered since 2018 across orthopaedics,
neurology, and general surgery.

**DEPARTMENT 1: ORTHOPAEDICS & TRAUMATOLOGY** *(Core Specialty ---
Primary Offering)*

This is the flagship department and the main reason patients seek out
ADUS Klinik. It covers the full musculoskeletal system, from
conservative (non-surgical) treatment through to complex surgical
procedures.

**Medical Director:** Prof. Dr. med. Christoph Erggelet ---
internationally renowned in knee and hip surgery; Professor of
Orthopaedics at the University of Freiburg; author of more than 150
specialist publications.

**Chief of Orthopaedics:** PD Dr. med. Patrick Vavken --- leading expert
in shoulder and elbow surgery; author of numerous scientific
publications.

**1a. Shoulder & Elbow**

Focus areas: shoulder prosthetics and revision prosthetics, rotator cuff
injuries, shoulder instability, biceps tendon pathologies,
tennis/golfer\'s elbow (epicondylitis), shoulder arthroscopy, elbow
surgery.

Key procedures: arthroscopic stabilisation, tendon repair, joint
replacement (total and partial), minimally invasive keyhole surgery.

Lead specialist: PD Dr. med. Patrick Vavken (upper extremities team
lead), Dr. med. Jana Jensen (shoulder prosthetics specialist, active in
sports orthopaedics).

**1b. Hip**

Focus areas: hip osteoarthritis (coxarthrosis), hip arthroplasty
(partial and total hip replacement), hip arthroscopy, cartilage damage,
bursitis, tendon disorders.

Key procedures: total hip replacement, partial joint replacement,
minimally invasive hip arthroscopy.

Lead specialist: Prof. Dr. med. Christoph Erggelet (lower extremities
team lead).

**1c. Knee**

Focus areas: knee osteoarthritis, primary knee prosthetics, ACL
(anterior cruciate ligament) tears and reconstruction, meniscus tears,
meniscus transplantation, cartilage damage and repair.

Key procedures: knee replacement (full and partial), ACL reconstruction
using autologous tendons (hamstring, quadriceps, patellar), meniscus
repair and transplantation, arthroscopic cartilage treatment.

Cartilage repair methods offered: microfracturing, mosaic plasty (OATS),
osteochondral transplantation (OCT), cartilage refixation.

Special note: ADUS Klinik has a particular institutional focus on
cartilage preservation and joint-preserving arthritis therapy --- a
direct reflection of the medical leadership\'s specialist background.
This is a key differentiator versus other clinics.

**1d. Foot & Ankle**

Focus areas: foot and ankle arthroscopy, cartilage lesions
(osteochondral lesions), ankle impingement syndrome, Achilles tendon
disorders, plantar fasciitis (heel spur), ankle ligament injuries,
deformity corrections.

Key procedures: ankle arthroscopy (keyhole surgery), cartilage repair,
ligament reconstruction, corrective procedures.

Lead specialist: Dr. med. Péter Kvarda --- Senior Physician
Orthopaedics, Head of Foot & Ankle Surgery. Exchange fellowship at
Aspetar Orthopaedic and Sports Medicine Hospital, Doha (Qatar).

**1e. Spine (Wirbelsäule)**

Focus areas: spinal pathologies, degenerative spine conditions, spinal
surgery where indicated.

Part of the broader orthopaedic and surgical offering at ADUS Klinik.

**1f. Fractures & Trauma (Traumatologie)**

Focus areas: acute fractures, broken bones (hand, ankle, wrist, lower
leg), post-traumatic care, fracture fixation, surgical trauma
management.

Lead specialist: Dr. med. Katarina Schär --- Senior Physician Surgery,
specialist in general and trauma surgery with 10+ years of experience at
a Level-A hospital. Specialisation in lower limb and general fractures.

**DEPARTMENT 2: CONSERVATIVE ORTHOPAEDICS & SPORTS MEDICINE**

Non-surgical treatments for musculoskeletal conditions, sports injuries,
and overuse damage. Also covers paediatric and adolescent sports
medicine (from age 6).

**Key treatments offered:**

**PRP Therapy (Platelet-Rich Plasma / Eigenbluttherapie)** Biological,
non-surgical treatment that accelerates healing of sports injuries and
overuse damage. Also effective in early-stage osteoarthritis. One
treatment session; patient\'s own blood is processed and re-injected
into the affected area.

Indications: Runner\'s knee, jumper\'s knee, partial ligament tears,
muscle fibre tears, early knee/hip/shoulder arthritis, Achilles
tendinopathy, plantar fasciitis, ankle ligament injuries.

**Hyaluronic Acid Injections** Joint injections to reduce pain and
improve joint mobility in osteoarthritis patients. Protects cartilage
and slows joint degeneration. Recommended: 3--5 injections one week
apart; follow-up after 9--12 months if needed. Note: often not fully
covered by basic health insurance; out-of-pocket cost likely.

Indications: Mild to moderate knee, hip, or shoulder osteoarthritis
where joint replacement is not yet required.

**Sports Medicine Consultations** Outpatient consultations for sports
injuries, performance-related conditions, and prevention. Covers
athletes of all levels --- from recreational to competitive --- and
young athletes from age 6. International experience in team medicine
(ice hockey, football, beach volleyball).

**DIAGNOSTIC TECHNOLOGY**

**3D Imaging (CBCT --- Cone Beam Computed Tomography)** Available
on-site in the consultation rooms since May 2023. Enables faster and
more precise diagnostics of joints, and improved post-operative outcome
monitoring. This is a direct competitive advantage --- patients receive
high-quality 3D joint imaging without needing a separate referral
elsewhere.

All standard imaging, physiotherapy, and radiological services are
available within the Gesundheitszentrum Dielsdorf building complex ---
everything under one roof.

**WHAT THE MARKETING AGENT SHOULD KNOW ABOUT THE OFFERINGS**

1.  **Cartilage & joint preservation is the institutional signature.**
    This is not just another joint replacement clinic. The leadership
    built their careers on saving joints --- surgical replacement is the
    last resort, not the first option.

2.  **The clinic treats patients from age 6** --- young athletes and
    paediatric sports injuries are explicitly part of the offering.

3.  **Conservative treatments (PRP, hyaluronic acid)** are genuine
    differentiators for patients who are not yet ready or suitable for
    surgery. These are content-rich topics for marketing.

4.  **3D imaging on-site** is a concrete, tangible advantage worth
    highlighting in content.

5.  **Do not promise specific outcomes or recovery times** in marketing
    content --- always frame conservatively (\"typically,\" \"in most
    cases,\" \"your doctor will advise\").

6.  **Insurance note:** Not all treatments are covered by basic Swiss
    health insurance (Grundversicherung). PRP and hyaluronic acid
    injections often involve out-of-pocket costs. Do not make claims
    about coverage in marketing content.
